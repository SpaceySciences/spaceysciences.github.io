The SpaceyScope Is now in Alpha

The high tech, easy to use telescope we've been working on is now in a limited alpha production run, the price starts at $350. It's sleek, simple, and super functional. The revolutionary electromagnetic control system makes it seem like magic when you direct your SpaceyScope with your phone.




Buy An Alpha


Images & Live Streams
In Your Palm

The SpaceyScope App will allow you to take images of any point in the sky. A single tap of the image button takes an image! You also have the ability to perform long exposures and time lapses, as well as a video stream. The SpaceyScope is capable of being controlled over bluetooth and wifi. The devices is 1 x 1 x 1 feet.

Easy to Share
Easy to Edit

Astrophotography is easier than ever with the SpaceyScope. You can share and edit your images from within the App! Our software will make it easy for you to extract beautiful details hidden in the night sky.

Your Space Guide
Explore the Night Sky

Our space guide lets you explore the night sky like never before. You have an entire sky map to search through and the ability to image anything you see on your map. Your skymap is automatically adjusted for your local light pollution, so you can know what you will see. In some cases the SpaceyScope will be able to see what you can't!

Explore Away
It's Never Been Easier

If you see an object you want to explore, with the simple tap of a button your SpaceyScope will navigate to that object. You can also place your phone to the sky and the app will automatically open the star map. From the star map you can select an object to learn more, or have your SpaceyScope navigate to it!
