Scientific Space Systems

Scientific Space Systems is a startup space company dedicated to bringing space closer to the public. Scientific Space Systems was formed in 2017 with the goal of building high-tech and easy to use telescopes for popular astronomical purposes. Scientific Space Systems's Telescopes (Formerly Spacey Telescopes) have already won several awards and have been featured on Atlanta Tech Spotlights, The University of Georgia , NPR Radio Broadcast, TEDx Talk, Major League Hacking, and more.
